Our World-Class Ferris Wheel! It’ll blow your mind.

So far, all you have heard about were our roller coaster rides and yes, they are great! However, we are particularly excited about our Ferris Wheel. Standing tall at an impressive height, our Ferris Wheel offers breathtaking views of the entire amusement park and beyond. As you ascend to the top, you'll be greeted with panoramic vistas that showcase the beauty of our park and the surrounding landscape. And, it's a ride for everyone! Whether you’re seeking a romantic escape with a loved one or a fun family adventure, our Ferris Wheel is perfect for all ages. Each spacious gondola is designed for comfort, allowing you to sit back and enjoy the ride as you gently sway high above the park.

Our Go-Kart Tracks? Oh yeah, they are pretty good. See for yourself.

Get ready to rev your engines and feel the thrill of speed at our exhilarating Go-Kart track! Perfect for adrenaline junkies and families alike, our state-of-the-art go-karts offer an unforgettable racing experience. Zoom around the twists and turns of our expertly designed track, competing against friends and family as you race for the finish line. With karts suitable for all ages, everyone can join in on the fun! Whether you’re a seasoned racer or a first-time driver, our friendly staff ensures a safe and exciting adventure. So buckle up, hit the gas, and let the race begin at Fun-musement Park!

And lastly, our swing rides. Checkmate.

Experience the thrill of our Giant Swing Ride, one of the centerpieces of Fun-musement Park! Towering high above the park, this massive attraction features a unique tilting top that adds an exhilarating twist to the classic swing ride experience. As you ascend, feel the rush of adrenaline as the swings lift you into the air, offering stunning views of the park and beyond. The innovative tilting mechanism allows each swing to sway dramatically, creating a sensation of flight that will leave you breathless. Perfect for thrill-seekers and families alike, our Giant Swing Ride promises unforgettable moments filled with laughter and excitement. So gather your friends, buckle up, and get ready for a soaring adventure that will have you coming back for more!
