About Us

Here at Fun-Musement Park Corporation INC we value our customers and riders. We try to bring smiles to every child's face, just like this child to the right! Home to 18 roller coaster rides and countless other activities in the heart of New York, we serve over a million customers each month. We are available with customer support 24/7 and work tirelessly to ensure the satisfaction of our customers.

In addition to the exemplary leadership from our executive board, we volunteer with local animal shelters to ensure a great life for everyone. We believe everyone on Earth deserves a good life, not just humans. We are also strongly committed to fighting climate change and have donated millions of dollars over the years. Press 'Learn More About Our Mission' to learn more about how we are working to better our community!
